Section 1

LIST OF CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE
Crisostomo Ibarra
  • Son of captain Rafael Ibarra, a young man of Spanish and Filipino decent with a wealthy background.
  • He plays the role of the male lead in the story, and his actions were inspired by Jose Rizal's real life experience.
  • Studied in Spain for 7 years, before returning to the Philippines.
Maria Clara
  • Daughter of captain Tiago and Dona Pia, grew under the supervision of Tiya Isabel.
  • She plays the role of the female lead in the story, her image represents the ideal Filipino woman.
  • Jose Rizal based her off one of his girlfriends, his cousin Leonor Rivera.
Padre Damaso
  • He was the former curate of the church in San Diego, but he was forced to move due to an inciddent.
  • He plays the role of the main antagonist in the story, he represents how corrupt the church and priests were back then.
  • He is described to be cruel and ruthless, and physically abusive.

Section 2

Don Rafael Ibarra
  • He is the father of Crisostomo Ibarra, he was fromerly the richest man in San Diego, owning lots of properties
  • He was known to be kind and generous.
  • His last wish to Crisostomo, was to build a school to teach children.
> Kapitan Tiago
  • He was supposed to marry off Maria Clara to Crisostomo Ibarra, but was forced to cancel it.
  • Valued social connections and himself more than anything.
  • Quick to obbey to his supperiors and generally meek, always obbeying orders from corrupt friars.
Elias
  • Was a fugitive living in San Diego, his mother dying when he was young and being a twin to his sister.
  • Was born into a rich family and lived hapilly with his grandfather, but was caught up into a scanadal.
  • He renounced his wealth and ran away with his father and twin sister.

Section 3

Padre Salvi
  • Was sent as the replacement for the church in San Diego, after Padre Damaso was sent away.
  • Liked imposing cash fines and penalties.
  • Was the competing rival of Padre Damaso.
Pilosopo Tasyo o Don Anastacio
  • He came from a wealthy family, and formally studied philosophy in a school.
  • His mother cut his education short out of fear of making him too smart and lose faith in religion.
  • He married but lost his wife early on. He would then work as the advisor for Don Rafael and Cissostomo Ibarra.
Sisa
  • Married to Pedro and is the mother of Basilio and Crispin.
  • She was part of a rich and wealthy family, but her husband used all of her money and sold all her jewelry to gamble.
  • She was a caring mother, but when Crispin was killed and she was being threathened to reveal Basilio's wareabouts she went insane.

Section 4

Crispin
  • Is one of the sons of Sisa, he was a young 8 year old boy who worked in the church as bell ringer along with his brother in San Diego.
  • He was falsely accused of stealing along with his brother, because of this he was beaten up and killed.
Basilio
  • Is one of the sons of Sisa, and the brother of Crispin
  • Unlike Crispin, he was able to flee from the church and managed to not get killed.
  • He was later taken in by Captian Tiago.
Alperes
  • Husband of Donya Consolacion
  • A high ranking commander in San Diego.
  • Often had fueds with Padre Salvi, they would often order henchmen to get in each others way.

Section 5

Donya Victorina de Espadaña
  • Wife of Don Tiburcio, he helped convinced people that he was a speciallized doctor who could cure all diseases.
  • She rejected all her suitors and since she need to marry in order to keep her status she married to the fraud doctor Don Tiburcio.
Don Tiburcio de Espadaña
  • Husband of Doña Victorina
  • He moved to the Philippines from Spain to practice medicine.
  • Would charge a hefty and unfair fee for his services, he would be later caught for his fraud.
Tiya Isabel
  • Took care of Maria Clara for most of her life.
  • Captain Tiago's sister.
Donya Consolacion
  • The wife of Alperes
  • Came from a poor family, worked as a laundry worker.
  • Liked to used alot of makeup.
